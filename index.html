Who is Braxton Coats?

A Brief Biography

Hey I'm Braxton! The purpose of this section is for you to get to know a little bit more about me, my life and things I love.

I'm from Raleigh, North Carolina and I love technology. I attended Sanderson High School from 2010-2014 and that is where I first discovered my obsession with technology. Being enrolled in a technical class during my sophmore year really opened my eyes to the world of programming since we were tasked with building a simple robot and then writing some code to make it pick up an object. Once I saw how powerful and interesting programming was, I decided to get my feet wet with designing a website. My first webpage was just a simple wordpress site to share some of my other nerdy loves, and it showed me just how deep and artistic web design can be.


Since highschool, I've been working my way to a BS in Computer Science at Appalachian State University. There, I've been learning about all aspects of computer science from the Java programming language, to the Y86 processor, and the construction of databases, just to name a few. At Appalachian, I am hoping to refine my skills in not only web design, but in all areas of programming, to become a skilled employee that has the knowledge and dedication to help design, implement or help applications that my employer uses.
